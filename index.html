Mission

We bring optimal care to every patient to reduce surgical errors and complications. Our vision is to create an equilibrium of surgical knowledge for operating room teams to deliver exceptional care every time.


Overview

Surgery is dynamic. Surgeons repeatedly adjust course of action in surgery to keep patients safe and achieve the most favorable outcome. What if validating that course of action with another doctor was only a tap away? Enter: Scrub In. Our network for surgeons and other surgical team members brings connectivity across operating rooms (OR) to work through friction points during surgery. Get a second opinion from another expert in any specialty and be better equipped to serve your patient. All in real time.

How it works


Case A: You are halfway through your call shift when a trauma rolls through and you decide the patient needs to go to the OR. As the surgery continues, you determine it’s beneficial to ask a colleague how to adjust a technique for this specific patient. All your colleagues are operating elsewhere or not available.

Who can you turn to in that moment?
